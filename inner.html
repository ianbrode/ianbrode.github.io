Unique formulation

The trend in microbial identification is to decrease the time of analysis while maintaining or improving the ability to identify different microorganisms. Identification based on microbial enzyme profiles provides the basis for a truly rapid system.

Diatabs offers an accurate and rapid identification of clinically relevant bacteria and yeasts. The concept of individual tests -used as single tests or in combination - offers the user many advantages over traditional methods.
